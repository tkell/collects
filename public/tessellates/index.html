tessellates

about

Tessellates is a few things, right now. It's a personal project to organize my own music collection that has gone a bit rogue. It's a better display for music -- much better than the spreadsheet music views typically on offer. It wants to be a delight to use, somewhere between Nintendo and Apple, heavy on the Nintendo. If you're a certain kind of music nerd who wants to spend more time with the music you have, tessellates will probably be for you.

goals

Tessellates has big dreams

  • imports from major streaming services (Spotify, Apple, etc)
  • imports from indie darlings (Discogs, Rekordbox, etc)
  • playback from same
  • stats
  • release annotations
